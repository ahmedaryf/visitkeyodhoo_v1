VAAVU KEYODHOO

Keyodhoo is a small and a beautiful island located in Vaavu Atoll (WOW Atoll). The island is about 1 hour and 30 minutes (79km) away from Velana International Airport and the Capital City Male’ by speedboat. Fishing and Local Tourism are the main source of income for Keyodhoo. The island population is approximately 800 people and the people are extremely friendly.

SNORKELING

Snorkeling and diving are among the top things to do in the Maldives. Keyodhoo has a beautiful house reef, you'll be able to go directly from the beach. If you're traveling on a budget, staying at a guesthouse in Keyodhoo you'll still be able to enjoy the marine life in the Maldives

VAAVU SHIPWRECK

The shipwreck in Vaavu Atoll Maldives is Absolutely amazing. The Shipwreck is one of the most Instagram worthy spots in the Maldives and it's just a few kilometers away from Keyodhoo Island

OCEAN PIZZA AND BAKERY

A place that you must visit! It’s the cutest little spot with amazing food, drinks and authentic ITALIAN delicious Pizzas. The ambiance is very welcoming and charming. Absolutely amazing place to have a cup of coffee.

CAFE ATHIRI BAA

Do youself a favor and visit this lovely Café The service is excellent and the atmosphere here is great. Hightly recommended!

ROYAL TASTE

Searching for a place to have a drink in Keyodhoo Island? ROYAL TASTE is the perfect place and various baverages are available. A great place to hangout.

KEYODHOO MOSQUE

Islam is the religion of the Maldives. Islam is both a religion and a complete way of life.
Mosque is a place of prayer for Muslims, Mosques are the heart of the Islamic life.

SPEEDBOAT TRANSFER

Speedboats are the most popular kind of transportation to get your destination in the Maldives. GOMA and DREAM SPEED are the most popular transportation services to Vaavu Atoll
